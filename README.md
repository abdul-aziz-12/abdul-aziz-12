# 💫 About Me:
About Me<br>
I am a data-driven Product Manager and Business Intelligence professional completing my MSc in Business Intelligence and Analytics at Clermont School of Business, with a proven track record of turning complex data problems into measurable business outcomes. At S&P Global, I led the SNL Feeds product line securing over 1 million dollars in revenue, drove 20 client adoptions through Snowflake and Marketplace expansion, boosted delivery efficiency by 40 percent, cut feed failures by 60 percent, managed AWS cloud migrations, oversaw 500 plus client transitions via Python automation, and ran 50 plus annual enterprise consultations translated into BRDs and PRDs. My technical stack spans SQL and Python (Pandas, PySpark, NumPy, Matplotlib), Power BI with DAX, MicroStrategy, OLAP, Snowflake, AWS (S3, FSx, Redshift), Azure, CI/CD, NoSQL, Bash, n8n, Zapier, LLM-powered workflows, Gemini API, Ethereum on-chain analytics via Dune, Allium, Ethplorer API, DeFi flow and whale movement tracking, plus Jira, Salesforce, WSJF, A/B testing, financial modeling, Figma, and JavaScript. Beyond the technical side, I bring strong cross-functional collaboration, stakeholder management, Agile/Scrum leadership as a certified Scrum Master, and the ability to communicate data findings to both technical and executive audiences.<br><br>
Future Targets and Direction<br>
I am focused on building and shipping AI-powered data products combining LLMs with structured BI pipelines, leading product strategy for cloud-native and Web3-adjacent platforms, and deepening expertise in real-time data architectures, ML-driven decision systems, and agentic AI workflows using n8n and MCP-based orchestration. The projects in this portfolio reflect that direction, spanning automation pipelines, on-chain analytics, data modeling, and end-to-end BI systems built with a product-first mindset. I am actively seeking Product, Data, or Consulting roles across France and the broader EMEA region, with immediate relocation availability, looking to combine strategic ownership with hands-on technical execution.
